Research & Innovation

An Integrated Smart System for Cashew Cultivation

Healthy trees, stronger yields. Empowering farmers with AI-driven insights for soil health, nut grading, and precision disease management.

Accuracy 98.5%

Research Domain

Addressing the critical challenges in modern cashew farming through data-driven integration.

Introduction to the Problem

Industry Scale: Sri Lanka's plantation sector relies heavily on manual labor, leading to quality variations.
Challenges: Labor shortages and limited mechanization reduce supply chain value.
Errors: Inaccurate disease detection and subjective nut grading cause financial losses.
Stakeholders: Impacts over 500,000 smallholder farmers lacking data-driven tools.

Research Gap

Most existing solutions are limited to lab environments or isolated functions. There is a significant lack of explainable AI models for real Sri Lankan field conditions, multilingual support for rural farmers, and alignment with technical guidelines for standardized grading.

Main Objective

To develop an Integrated Smart System for Cashew Cultivation—a hybrid AI-IoT platform automating soil health analysis, disease detection, pest identification, and quality grading. The goal is to improve efficiency, standardize nut quality for export, and reduce manual errors.

Solution Overview

A hybrid, trilingual AI-IoT system providing real-time insights in under 5 seconds utilizing a Flask API and Flutter app.

CNN & YOLOv8 IoT Sensors Flask & Flutter Sinhala, Tamil, English

Core Components

Our integrated system is built on four revolutionary pillars, combining advanced sensing with AI intelligence.

Soil Analysis & Health Scoring

Real-time analysis of NPK levels (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium), pH, and moisture using integrated IoT sensors to provide localized health scores.

Learn more

Automated Cashew Nut Grading

Deep learning vision system to classify nuts into Sri Lankan standard grades based on precise size, color, and texture algorithms.

Learn more

Disease Detection & Remedies

Detection of diseases like Anthracnose and Gummosis using leaf and nut imaging, followed by biological remedy recommendations.

Learn more

Pest & Pesticide Recommendation

Early warning systems for Stem Borers and Tea Mosquito Bugs with precise pesticide dosage calculations to minimize chemical use.

Learn more
